About Us

We are a team of Santa Clara University students that are looking to help bay area college students find the best food on their campus. We know that dining hall food options can be expensive and daunting, so we hope this web application can be useful!

FAQ

You can always send us a request on the contact page, and we can review the school. Once we get the school published, we will send you an email invite to try it out!

Using our app is straightforward. Start by selecting your school from the dropdown menu on the main page. You can then browse the dining hall options, read reviews, and leave your own ratings. If you have any questions or run into any issues, feel free to contact us through the contact page.

To submit dining hall ratings, simply navigate to the dining hall you wish to rate and click on the "Leave a Rating" button. You will be prompted to provide your rating and any comments you have about the dining experience. Once submitted, your rating will be visible to other users.

We take your privacy seriously. Our app only collects information necessary for providing the service, such as your email for account creation and communication. We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy page. Our location policy ensures that we only track location data when necessary for providing location-based services, and you have full control over your location settings.
